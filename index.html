About Me

Transforming ideas into intelligent solutions

I am a data scientist and Machine Learning researcher with a proven track record of applying cutting-edge AI to solve real-world challenges. With dual Master's degrees from UBC in Computer Science and Data Science, I bring both theoretical depth and practical expertise to every project.
